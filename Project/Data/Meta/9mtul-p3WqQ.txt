May 1st, 2013- 
So, in 1993 when I was in 4th grade, my class put on a Christmas skit in which I did a lip sync to the song in this video. My dad recorded the skit, and because my family thought that it was really funny, we watched it over and over. But, three or four years later, we lost the video. We searched and searched for years until we decided that it was just gone for good. 

Then in 2006, I decided that I would recreate the video for my mom as a Christmas present. It took me a while to find the song, but eventually I did, and I recreated the video. My mom loved it, and it has been enjoyed by many over the last 6 years.

This past Sunday, April 28th, 2013, my mom briefly stopped in town for a visit. We went to a coffee shop, and my mom brought her computer because she said she had something to show me. She fiddled for a minute and then turned up the volume. It was the video from my 4th grade class. She contacted my 4th grade teacher who still had a copy of the skit, and made a copy for me. I have the best mom ever!!! Here's the link to the original performance. Enjoy!!!
