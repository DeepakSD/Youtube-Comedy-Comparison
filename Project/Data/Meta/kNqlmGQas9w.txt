Wallace Spearmon, Jr., (born December 24, 1984 in Chicago, Illinois, USA) is a sprint athlete who specializes in the 200 meters. He is a two-time NCAA outdoor champion in the 200 m and won the silver medal in the event at the 2005 World Championships in Athletics. He has a personal best of 19.65 seconds for the distance, making him the fifth fastest runner of all-time and he also holds the indoor American record.

He has won the bronze medal twice at the World Championships in Athletics in 2007 and 2009.
