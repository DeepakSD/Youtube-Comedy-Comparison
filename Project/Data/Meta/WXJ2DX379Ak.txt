The Collectibles is a superhero comedy webseries where Superfriends meets The Office. 

In the wake of a teammate's career-ending injury, the world's foremost super team, The Power Posse, must navigate office politics and leverage their merchandising potential to appease their corporate sponsors. When the corporate honchos promise to shift the team management paradigm, is it just a warning or is Super Star's status as team leader in jeopardy? Is this Ultrafemme's chance to take over leadership at last? Will Death-Wish's conspiracy theories prove true? Will Aguaman ever be taken off the injured reserve list? Will The Quick put down his coffee? Will Receiver get her chance in the field? And will Shield Maiden stop texting for just one damn minute?
