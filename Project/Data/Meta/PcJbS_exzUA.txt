After performing to standing-room-only crowds at their June première, the QUEER COMEDY COLLECTIVE (or "QCC") -- Canada's first and only queer sketch comedy troupe -- is back for an even bigger show at Toronto's Buddies in Bad Times Theatre. This must-see limited engagement runs for three nights only, October 27, 28 and 29, 2011. 

The QCC's latest show offers a satirical take on a variety of hot topics, including the obsession with celebrity, the conservative drift of Canadian politics, and the trendiness of gay adoption. The show also features some unforgettable characters, like Fischer's rye 'n ginger enhanced divorcée Evelyn Reese; Keller's campy Céline Dion; Zucker's painfully straight pride-parade commentator; and Dinn and Duff's hyper-aggressive Loyola and Terri-Lyn, stars of the fictitious realty TV series Newfie Shore.
