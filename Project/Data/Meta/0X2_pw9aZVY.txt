This is a promo video for the new comedy series, "Home at Last," premiering OCTOBER 25, 2011 on this channel.

The show stars William Russ (Boy Meets World) and features Jeff Lewis (The Guild). 

The character featured in this video is Pete, played by Eric Rubin. Pete is a little bit of a d bag.

In this epic fail type video, he really gets what he deserves. Seriously, if you're going to jump over a car, try to keep your eyes on the road.
