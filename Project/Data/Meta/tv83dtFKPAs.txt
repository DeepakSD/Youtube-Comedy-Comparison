The SH*Z glossary:
DTR = Define The Relationship. When two people like each other and aren't really sure what to do with it they "DTR" to figure things out. I guess that may only be a Utah Mormon thing haha :)
CTR = Choose The Right
EFY (songs) = EFY is a Mormon summer camp where kids go to meet each other and have fun. Whenever you go they give you a CD full of cheesy songs :3
Pre-mi = A mormon (usually boy) that hasn't served a mission yet, and therefore basically has no chance of getting married.
Word Of Wisdom = The Word Of Wisdom is a guideline followed by all LDS members! It's a list of things that they are strongly recommended not to do. It tells us not to drink, smoke, do stuff like that. Along with this it includes eating excessive amounts of food. Basically binging is against it as well.
