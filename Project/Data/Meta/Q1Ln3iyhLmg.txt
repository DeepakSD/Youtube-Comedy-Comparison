COMEDY!
MONTREAL | NOVEMBER 20TH, 2011 | AT 6:00PM

Humour will take centre stage this November when Team USA challenges Team Canada to a side-splitting duel of jokes and more jokes. The event aptly titled USA vs. CANADA sees four of the best comics from each country squaring off in a serious game of humour.

Team USA Captain, Dave Lester will have his work cut out for him competing against Team CANADA, who boast home-field advantage. With rookies like Chris Clarke aiming to hit it out of the park, Team USA will be playing for keeps.

Team CANADA is lead by the veteran and heavyweight comic, Jay Martin. Martin is one of the most recognisable on the Canadian comedy circuit. With his refreshing and inventive comedic style Jay is sure to catch USA off guard with some trick plays. Jay following his faith recently reinvented himself as the Christian Comic. His team has three of Canada's hardest hitting comics including Mark Trinidad and Jean Paul. Martin and Team CANADA are playing for nothing less than a clean knockout!

USA vs. CANADA is a battle for laughs featuring 8 of the best comics in North America. Comics at the top of their game, vying for bigger laughs? That's entertainment!

No matter which team you're betting on, there is one clear winner...comedy!
