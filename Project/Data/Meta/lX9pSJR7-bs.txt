Q: Were you guys hotboxing in the "Success" segment of the Behind the Scenes video?

A: We were not hotboxing. We never use drugs in writing or performing the show. But watching.... now that's another story. I recently received and email from a Boston man that told me he dropped some liquid acid and he FINALLY understood the show. We don't think you need any drugs to think it's funny, although it's pretty obvious that they can enhance the experience. Just like amazing music or art or making out, etc.
