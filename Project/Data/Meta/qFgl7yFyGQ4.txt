Hitler gets the news that the famous CEO and founder of Apple has died. Hitler may be a technophobe, but he loves "Toy Story", and even he is aware of the adverts for computers starring a bloke with a little moustache - and he has seen the people wearing little white wires in their hearing aids. Jobs invented a new kind of cassette, or something! . However, Hitler cannot believe that a major shareholder in Disney could possibly just pop off like that without turning into a robot and inventing something else we don't need, like iTV. This is sad news and may Steve Jobs rest in peace.
