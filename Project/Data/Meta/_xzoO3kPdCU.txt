His latest commercial is called "Good Doggie" for the 2012 Doritos Crash The Super Bowl contest, produced by Nicholas George and stars Mark Serritella. http://www.facebook.com/markserritella
 
Good Doggie also stars "Sasha", the dog who was abandoned and spending her days at a Humane Society animal shelter. Sasha didn't seem to have much chance of being rescued from the animal shelter because she was already past the cute puppy stage, and, as animals get older, the odds of them being put to sleep increase dramatically. Nobody would have guessed that Sasha would go from the lonely shelter life to potentially being seen by more than 100 million viewers, starring in "Good Doggie," a TV commercial shot for the Doritos® Crash the Super Bowl contest and produced by Nicholas George, author of "Film Crew" available at www.filmandvideoproduction.net

Sasha the rescue dog's performance in the Doritos® commercial contest entry has propelled her "Good Doggie" video into the Top 10 on the "Crash the Super Bowl" website. 

Good Doggie is about a loveable housedog who is home alone when an unwelcomed visitor sidesteps the alarm system and squeezes in through the doggie door. Unwittingly befriended by the intruder, Sasha follows him around the house as he helps himself to loose lying valuables. Everything appears to be going the burglar's way, until he sets his sights on one object in the house that is definitely off limits. Our normally docile, four-legged hero Sasha must now find a way to protect the home's treasures.
