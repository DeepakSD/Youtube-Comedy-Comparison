NEW INFO ON THE NEW ROBOCOP MOVIE!

What's director Jose Padilha's idea for the new RoboCop movie? He's spilled a few details...
Published on Nov 8, 2011


After being held up for some time in the well-reported financial woes of MGM these past few years, the planned new take on RoboCop finally seems to be pressing ahead. The appointment of a director earlier this year was a firm step forward, with Jose Padilha, the helmer of Elite Squad: The Enemy Within, being given the job.

Coming Soon caught up with him, too, to find out a little more as to what he had planned for the film.

"I have my take on it", he told the site.  "And I can tell you this: in the first RoboCop when Alex Murphy is shot, gunned down, then you see some hospitals and stuff and then you cut to him as RoboCop. My movie is between those two cuts. How do you make RoboCop? How do you slowly bring a guy to be a robot? How do you actually take humanity out of someone and how do you program a brain, so to speak, and how does that affect an individual?"

Padilha is hoping to start shooting the new movie at the start of next year, and with that in mind, he's got the small matter of a RoboCop to cast. Would he be looking towards Elite Squad lead Wagner Maura for the job? No, not he won't.

"We need an American RoboCop, man. RoboCop is an American guy, his name is Alex Murphy,"

So there you have it.
